Unit 3 (6 Hours)

Yard Maneuvers

Before hitting live traffic, you must master the physics of the motorcycle in a controlled environment.

Figure-8 / Slalom Simulation

Observe how the rider maintains a smooth trajectory around the cones, coordinating speed and balance.

Maneuver 01
Moving Off & Balance
Smoothly coordinate the clutch (left hand) and throttle (right hand) to start moving without stalling. Understand how speed creates gyroscopic stability.
Maneuver 02
Gear Changes
Shift up and down through the gears smoothly using the left foot lever, coordinating with the clutch to maintain control of the bike's momentum.
Maneuver 03
U-Turns & Figure Eights
Master low-speed steering. Crucial rule: Always turn your head and look at your intended destination ‚Äî the motorcycle goes where your eyes look.
Maneuver 04
Hard / Emergency Braking
Achieve optimum braking without locking the wheels. Apply progressive pressure to the front brake (~70% power) and light pressure to the rear (~30%), pulling the clutch just before stopping.

Navigating live traffic requires constant observation, anticipation, and correct positioning to "see and be seen".
